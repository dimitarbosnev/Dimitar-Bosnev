What is Magic Chess?

Magic Chess is a variation of Chess or a 1 versus 1 player game where players take turns moving their pieces in order to capture the opposing player’s King.

What is Magic Chess trying to achive?

The vast accumulation of knowledge people have about Chess has reached a point where every possible move has been analyzed either by a person or a computer. This has led to a stagnation in innovation, making mastery reliant on memorizing an exhaustive set of moves. Aspiring players find playing chess at a higher level unrewarding, as the game is predominantly played in a repetitive and unvarying manner.

Magic Chess aims to disrupt the repetitive nature of traditional Chess, fostering an environment that promotes player expression. Magic Chess introduces dynamic elements for unpredictability, enhancing the overall gaming experience. This innovative approach encourages players to showcase their creativity and strategic prowess, making each move a unique expression of their style and adaptability.

Design Choice

When I was thinking about the feel of the game, I wanted to feel like the player was playing a chess game from another magical world. This is why I decided to make the board from hexagons and the main theme to be a forest elven theme.

Magic Chess's unique selling point is the magic moves pieces have. There are additional options that players have to execute their tactics. My philosophy for balancing these moves is that they should aid the player in winning but they shouldn’t result in a win directly.

Magic Chess Iteration 2

In this iteration I had to rewrite a lot of the code in order to accomodate all chess pieces and their special moves. The main added feature this time around is the multiplayer using a client server architecture. When running the game, the player would immediately get paired with another player who is waiting to join a game. My goal for future iterations is adding UI, improving the player expirence so they can first join a chat lobby, making the server authoritive and being able to play against bots.
