What is Concordia

Concordia Art & Culture Foundation is a public benefit organization dedicated to promoting and preserving art and culture across various communities. It engages in activities such as organizing exhibitions, cultural events, educational programs, and supporting artists.

What is The Monster Project?

Concordia had asked a number of children to illustrate their own monsters and write something about them. Concordia as our client had tasked me and my team to bring these monsters to life in a child-frendly game.

What our game is about?

We decided to create a VR game where these kids can interact with their monsters in a more immersive way. For most of the kids this was going to be their first time trying VR. With that in mind we wanted to provide them with an amazing experience.

About the game

The game was made in Unity. What this game stood out with was the unique movement where the player can use their hands to move around and even jump high in the air. The most challenging part was implementing climbing up structures as it would cause a lot of camera shake. In the end I was able to fix that and provide a smooth experience when climbing.

What did I learn from it?

This was my first time developing a VR game. And I was able to utilize the majority of the features of the Unity XR toolkit and gain valuable experience in VR development.
